Condesa Typography


  • Title Font: CLIMATE CRISIS VARIABLE

  • Title Font Weight: REGULAR

  • Title Font Style: REGULAR


  • Subheading & Text Font: HELVETICA NEUE WORLD

  • Subheading & Text Font Weight: BOLD

  • Subheading & Text Font Style: HELVETICA NEUE WORLD 75 BOLD



Condesa Palette


  • ec5027 (RED-ORANGE TEXT AND BACKGROUND)

  • f5ce45 (YELLOW BACKGROUND)

  • 3176b5 (BLUE BACKGROUND)

  • f3f1e0 (TAN TEXT AND BACKGROUND)

  • 1d1d1d (BLACK TEXT)




Other Design Elements


  • Columns: 3 (MENU BAR), 1 (TITLE AND MAIN PAGE), 2 (MAIN PAGE CONT. AND YELLOW BACKGROUND), 1 (BLUE BACKGROUND), 1 (FOOTER TITLE), 3 (CTA AND SUBSCRIPTION PAGE)


  • Images: Large Hero Image (MAIN PAGE), 2 Medium Images (MAIN PAGE CONT.), 1 Medium Image (YELLOW BACKGROUND)


  • Interactive Items: 4 Links + Shopping Cart Button + "Inquire Now" Button (MENU BAR), 3 Links (MAIN PAGE + MAIN PAGE CONT.), "Explore My Services" Button (YELLOW BACKGROUND), 6 Links + 2 Text boxes + "Send Away" Button (CTA AND SUBSCRIPTION PAGE)
